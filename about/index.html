DopeLists

DopeLists are curated repositories of categorized high quality websites which are divided by various fields of interests. At first I created this site for my own private use because I wanted to have my bookmarked web design sites in a place where I can get to from anywhere and any computer. After making web design category I got an idea that I could also categorize other things that I'm interested in:

Web Design — The most useful sites in a world of web design and development.

After Format — Download links to software which usually gets installed on fresh Windows install.

Indie Games — Worth noting independent games.

Free 2 Play — All the good games that are free to play.

Browser Games — Just the best and most addicting browser games.

Other — Cool sites that don't belong to any category.
